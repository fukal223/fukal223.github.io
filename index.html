AHIMSA = DO NOT HARM

A guiding principal of yoga is ahimsa. This translates as non-violence in thought, word or deed. This principal is applied to all sentient beings.

LIVE WITH COMPASSION

It's the most influential change we can make. It effects billions of farming animals, the health of our planet, and every being who lives here.

TOGETHER WE CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Around 300 million yogis practice yoga world wide. The yoga community has the power to raise the collective consciousness.

GO VEGAN

Align your values with your actions. Every time we eat, we have the choice to embrace love.
