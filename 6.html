Yoga Teacher in London

I am a yoga teacher 200RYT (yoga alliance).

Fan of movement and creativity for many years, I have been exploring different artistic and corporal disciplines since a very young age, such as sculpture, dance and yoga.

A few years ago, when I first rolled out my yoga mat, I thought I would be discovering a discipline which would allow me to relax and improve my flexibility. However, yoga has given me a lot more than that. It has become an important part of my life. For me, it is a way of living, listening and learning. It is a great source of happiness that I have the pleasure to share.

I am also very passionate about vegan food. My progress towards a plant-based diet has been done step by step. It is a diet that is in accordance with my values ​​and provides me all the energy I need for my physical activities.

I regularly post on my website recipes that are easy to follow, which I hope will inspire you.
